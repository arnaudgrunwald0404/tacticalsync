# H1 2026 Rallying Cry, Defining Objectives & Strategic Initiatives

## Rallying Cry — H1 2026

> **Fuel customer growth and retention through disciplined execution**

---

## DO #1 — Improve Operational Efficiency & Discipline Across the Company

**Definition**

Building and strengthening foundations to provide clarity and resources to enable individual success and cross-functional collaboration that drive improved business outcomes consistently.

**Primary Success Metric**

* OpEx management and achievement of SI-level metrics.

### Strategic Initiatives

1. **Process Standardization & Optimization**

   * Create and roll out templated SOPs for critical roles and processes, including hand-offs.
   * Identify and address 3–5 of the most critical process gaps (e.g., escalation paths, use of short-term agencies/vendors, deal desk, product sunsetting, pre-sale/sale/implementation readiness, tracking product commitments on SOWs).

2. **Drive Resource Efficiency**

   * Successfully execute the H1 rightshoring/techshoring plan.
   * Ensure the right talent is in the right roles via semi-annual talent reviews and gap assessments.
   * Provide the right tools and resources so teams can achieve company goals effectively.

3. **Create a Data-Driven Decision-Making Culture**

   * Improve clean data collection and reportability.
   * Establish continuous feedback and improvement loops.
   * Identify 3–5 key areas to tackle first (via survey to XLT).

---

## DO #2 — Improve Customer Retention

**Definition**

We aim to predict, prevent, and intervene on customer risk to improve customer retention and drive increased revenue.

**Primary Success Metric**

* Leading indicators of churn (customer health, adoption score, etc.) — focus on which indicators we can move in H1 via the initiatives below.

### Strategic Initiatives

1. **Product-Related Churn Taskforce**

   * Improve usage data availability and visibility into adoption gaps.
   * Establish a triage/prioritization process for product issues (including value and risk).
   * Create and execute a plan to address system slowness and data infrastructure issues.

2. **Integrations Taskforce**

   * Focus on integration-related drivers of churn and prioritize remediation.

3. **Org-Wide Customer Health & Risk Scoring**

   * Define and roll out a standard Customer Health Score, Risk Score, Sentiment Score, and associated operational rigor.

---

## DO #3 — Strengthen ICP Bookings Performance

**Definition**

Improve bookings coming from ICP accounts by enhancing sales execution, pipeline creation, and conversion efficiency across the full sales cycle.

**Primary Success Metric**

* Increased win rate within ICP.

### Strategic Initiatives

1. **Further Define ICP & Evangelize Across the Company**

   * Produce a clear, documented ICP definition.
   * Deliver enablement so all teams understand and use ICP guidance.

2. **Increase Customer Advocacy Within ICP**

   * Grow ICP references, referrals, case studies, and reviews (# of ICP endorsements).

3. **Target ICP Accounts Across All Buying Stages**

   * Define and benchmark lifecycle stage progression.
   * Coordinate sales and marketing efforts around ICP accounts in active buying cycles.

---

## DO #4 — Improve Product for Predictable Delivery and Scalability

**Definition**

Strengthen our product delivery process so customers and internal teams can depend on it by driving predictable delivery (on schedule, with clear expectations, and minimal disruptions), scalability (handles growth and usage), and reliability (increased confidence with each release).

**Primary Success Metric**

* X% of planned product releases are delivered on schedule (to be defined) with no P1/P2 incidents or rollback required within X days of deployment.

### Strategic Initiatives

1. **Implement & Refine New Product Launch Readiness Process**

   * Define process, artifacts (including a go/no-go matrix), meetings, and communications.
   * Roll out by end of February, then practice and improve the process over the following four months.

2. **Improve Roadmap Visibility, Accountability & Change Management**

   * Run quarterly team roadmap meetings.
   * Send monthly roadmap email from CPO.
   * Maintain weekly roadmap change updates (Roadmap Visualizer).

3. **Deliver Solutions That Alleviate Integration Issues**

   * Reduce customer new-hire integration support tickets by X%.
   * Replace UKG Pro and/or define a clear migration plan by a specific date.
